ABOUT US

The Flower Power Cultural Association has organized concerts, shows, events for children, theater and film camps, various cultural projects, dedicated to children and teenagers, since its foundation (2013). Flower Power Cultural Association is a non-governmental association whose main objective is to support and promote young artists and their projects. The association has as its members actors, directors, cultural managers, young professionals and has set out to identify and apply new ways of using arts pedagogy, especially acting, in education.

Mission of the Flower Power Cultural Association

ACFP's mission is to create projects and programs that support the activities of young artists and the real communication between them and the other institutions and entities that can contribute to the development and running of their projects, as well as their involvement in various fields: education, organization of events, training professional, formulating cultural policies adapted to the international cultural context .

The First Breath project

In November 2021, the Vodafone Romania Foundation announced the winning projects of the annual funding round of the Life Fund for Newborns. Among the projects that were declared winners is the "First Breath" project . The project, proposed by the Flower Power Cultural Association, has now reached the final stage of implementation. The project received funding of 574,000 lei from the Vodafone Romania Foundation and had a total value of 638,113 RON. Its overall objective was to optimize the medical act and increase the degree of safety of newborns. The project facilitates the access of medical personnel to high-performance technology in the diagnostic activity and the development of medical procedures and aimed at the modernization of the neonatology section of the Slatina County Emergency Hospital.
